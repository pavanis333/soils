# Soils of India - UPSC Prep App

Comprehensive React app for UPSC CSE Geography preparation covering 8 major soil types of India as defined by Indian Council of Agriculture Research.

## 8 Soil Types Covered

1. **Alluvial Soil** (>40% area) - Most common, Bhangar & Khadar
2. **Black Soil (Regur)** - 3rd common, Cotton soil, self-ploughing
3. **Red and Yellow Soil** - Crystalline igneous, iron-rich
4. **Laterite Soil** - Acidic, plantation crops
5. **Arid/Desert Soil** - Sandy, saline, Indira Gandhi Canal
6. **Saline Soil (Usara)** - Salt-rich, White Revolution
7. **Peaty/Marshy Soil** - 40-50% organic content
8. **Forest & Mountain Soil** - Valley vs Upper slopes
